Fort Bragg, NC: The Canter of the Universe

Fort Bragg, NC is locate near the city of Fayetteville in the Sand Hills of North Carolina. The fort is affectionately call the "Center of the Universe" by past and present Paratrooper, because no matter where else you are stationed all roads lead to Bragg for a Paratrooper.
